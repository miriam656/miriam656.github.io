About:

Hello and welcome to my website! I'm Miriam Solis M., a PhD student in Mechanical and Manufacturing Engineering at the University of Calgary with a focus on Pipeline Engineering.

I'm passionate about the complex dynamics of fluid flow in pipelines and I'm dedicated to finding ways to optimize their performance and enhance their reliability.

On this website, you'll find information about my research, publications, and experience, as well as some insights into the exciting world of pipeline engineering. Whether you're a fellow researcher, a student, or simply interested in the field, I hope you find this website informative and engaging. Thank you for visiting and for your interest in get to know me!
